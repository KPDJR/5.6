Hardscaping vs. Landscaping: What Is the Difference?

Landscaping and hardscaping often overlap, but they are not the same. Understanding the difference can help you choose the right contractor and describe your project clearly.

Landscaping

Landscaping usually refers to living and natural elements such as lawns, shrubs, trees, garden beds, mulch, grading, and seasonal maintenance.

Hardscaping

Hardscaping refers to built outdoor features such as patios, walkways, retaining walls, steps, fire pits, edging, and stonework. These projects often require more planning, materials, and site preparation.

Which Contractor Do You Need?

For weekly lawn care, choose a maintenance-focused landscaper. For patios or walls, look for a company with hardscape experience and examples of similar completed projects.
