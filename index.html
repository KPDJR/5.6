Hiring help

Frequently asked questions

How much does landscaping cost in Massachusetts?

Costs vary by property size, scope, materials, timing, and access. Routine lawn care is usually priced differently than cleanups, planting, patios, walls, drainage, or full outdoor renovations.

What should I ask before hiring a landscaper?

Ask about insurance, recent similar work, written estimates, cleanup expectations, scheduling, materials, payment terms, and whether hauling or disposal is included.

Should I compare multiple local companies?

Yes. Comparing a few local providers helps you understand scope, communication style, pricing, availability, and whether the company is a good fit for your property.
